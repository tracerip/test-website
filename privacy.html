## Privacy Policy: Because we prioritize anonymity we don't store emails, users, or passwords when creating an account. You will instead be generated a unique alphanumeric account ID ( similar to [mullvad]() ). If you lose this ID we will not be able to help you recover your account. What we don't log: - No logging of traffic - No logging of DNS requests - No logging of IP addresses - No logging of user bandwidth - No logging of usernames, emails, and passwords To make sure you get full privacy due to the webhost logging IPs themself (we cant stop that unless we switch to sketchy webhosts) its recommended to use a vpn or tor and follow basic OPSEC guidelines.
